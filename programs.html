Our Programs

Four Programs. One Fight.

Every AHG program exists because young people are the subjects of decisions but never in the room when those decisions are made. Each program is a different door into the same fight: putting the next generation where they belong.

BRIDGE

Breaking Rules In Decisions that Govern Education

Ages 14 - 18

BRIDGE is AHG's school-based advocacy program for high school students. Participants learn to identify problems in their schools, build data-backed cases, and present them directly to the people who make decisions. This is not a suggestion box. This is structured power.

Learn More
SEAT

Students Earning A Table

Ages 16 - 24

SEAT trains the next generation of civic leaders to run for office, serve on boards, and occupy the decision-making spaces where their futures are determined. Real preparation for real authority. Every graduate leaves with a personal civic action plan and the skills to execute it.

Learn More
LAUNCH

Leading A United Next Generation CHange

Ages 18 - 26

LAUNCH serves young adults who are building from nothing. Financial literacy, career navigation, professional development, and the tools the system was supposed to provide but never did. Your zip code is your starting line, not your ceiling.

Learn More
FLOOR

Future Leaders On Real Reform

Ages 18 - 26 · Competitive

FLOOR is AHG's flagship policy fellowship. Competitively selected young adults are placed inside government offices and legislative chambers to do real work, build real relationships, and earn real credentials. This is for the people traditional fellowship programs never considered.

Learn More
