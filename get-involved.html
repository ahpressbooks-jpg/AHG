Get Involved

The Fight Needs More Than Money.

It needs people who show up, speak up, and refuse to look away. There is a role here for you.

Ways to Engage

Pick Your Fight.

01 — Volunteer

Give Your Time. Change the System.

AHG volunteers are not background extras. They are embedded in the work. We need people for program facilitation, community outreach, event logistics, and administrative operations.

If you have a skill and a spine, we have a role for you.

Apply to Volunteer
02 — Corporate Partnership

Align Your Brand with the Movement.

Corporate partnership with AHG is not a logo on a flyer. It is public alignment with a fight for the next generation. We offer three partnership tiers starting at $5,000/year, each designed for organizations that want to be on the right side of this moment.

Your employees are watching. Your customers are watching. Stand for something.

View Partnership Options
03 — Speak at AHG

Bring Your Voice to the Room.

AHG hosts events, panels, town halls, and summits throughout the year. We are looking for educators, policy advocates, and young leaders who have something real to say and the credibility to say it.

If you have lived it, studied it, or fought for it, we want you on our stage.

Submit a Speaker Inquiry
04 — Spread the Word

The Movement Grows When You Talk About It.

Follow us. Share our content. Tag the people in your community who need to know this exists. Every share, every conversation, every DM that says "look at this" is how movements scale.

You do not need a budget to change the reach of this fight.

Instagram Facebook

Stop Watching. Start Building.

The next generation is not waiting for permission. Neither should you.
