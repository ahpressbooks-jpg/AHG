Our Services

Strategic counsel for
work that cannot fail.

AHG delivers institutional-grade strategy, communications, and program development to organizations where the stakes are real and the margin for error is low. Every service is built around genuine partnership, not deliverable management.

01

Policy Research & Advocacy Strategy

We translate complex policy landscapes into clear, actionable strategy. AHG conducts primary and secondary research, stakeholder mapping, legislative analysis, and coalition-building plans that give advocates, organizations, and campaigns a real path from position to win.

This service is for organizations that know what they want to change but need a disciplined, evidence-grounded strategy to get there — built by people who understand both the research and the political reality.

$5,000 — $25,000 per engagement
02

Strategic Communications

Message architecture, narrative development, earned media strategy, and communications infrastructure built for campaigns, advocacy organizations, and institutions with something important to say. We don't do press releases — we build the architecture that makes every communication land.

From internal stakeholder messaging to public-facing campaigns, AHG builds communication systems that are consistent, compelling, and built to last beyond a single news cycle.

$3,500 — $15,000 per engagement
03

Program Development

Program design, logic model development, theory of change construction, and implementation planning — built with communities, not just for them. AHG helps organizations build programs that are both rigorous in design and grounded in the realities of the people they serve.

We work with nonprofits, foundations, and government agencies to develop programs that are fundable, implementable, and honest about what they can and cannot deliver.

$7,500 — $35,000 per engagement
04

Workforce Development Consulting

Curriculum development, employer engagement strategy, systems alignment, and program design for workforce pipelines serving underestimated communities. AHG brings together education, policy, and economic development expertise to build pathways that actually lead somewhere.

This service is for workforce boards, community colleges, nonprofits, and employer consortia that want to build pipelines with lasting economic impact — not just credential counts.

$5,000 — $20,000 per engagement
05

Education Consulting

Strategic support for school districts, charter organizations, school boards, and education reform advocates navigating policy, culture, community engagement, and institutional change. AHG brings a practitioner's understanding of how schools actually work alongside a strategist's view of where they need to go.

From district-level strategic planning to charter authorization strategy to board communications, we work with education institutions that are ready to be honest about the work ahead.

$4,000 — $18,000 per engagement
06

Retainer Advisory

Ongoing senior-level counsel for executives and organizations that need a trusted strategic partner available every month — not just when a crisis hits. AHG's retainer relationships provide consistent strategic guidance, real-time decision support, and an outside perspective that cuts through internal inertia.

Retainer clients receive priority access, monthly strategy sessions, and a partner who is deeply familiar with their organization and invested in their long-term success.

$2,500 — $8,000 per month

A note on pricing: The ranges listed reflect the scope and complexity of most engagements. Pricing is determined in partnership with each client based on organizational budget, scope of work, and timeline. AHG is committed to working with mission-driven organizations of different resource levels — if the work is right, we find a way. Reach out to begin a conversation.
